How did Bob Born, Father of Peeps, Dies ?Know his cause of death

July 2024 · 4 minute read

An executive with a confectionery firm, Ira “Bob” Born considered the “Father of Peeps” for automating the production of marshmallow chicks, sadly passed away on January 29th at 98 years. Born was the longtime leader of Just Born Quality Confections, a 100-year-old family business, which announced on Monday that he had passed away peacefully on the previous day.

Keep reading to know more about Bob Born, including who he was, how he died, what was the cause of his death, tributes for Bob Born, and much more!

Who Was Bob Born?

On September 29, 1924, Bob was born in New York City. His father, Sam Born, was a Russian immigrant who had established the tiny candy business Just Born Inc. in 1923. Later, the family relocated to Bethlehem, Pennsylvania, which is today the headquarters of Just Born.
Bob Born received his engineering physics degree from Lehigh University. He joined the U.S. Navy and was a lieutenant on a destroyer in the Pacific while working as a radar expert. Later, the Navy sent him to the Massachusetts Institute of Technology and the University of Arizona to pursue graduate studies in math and physics.

Journey to Become “Father of Peeps”:

Born was raised in a family of confectioners and followed in his family’s footsteps. He started working in his family’s candy store at a young age and eventually took over the business. With his passion for candy-making, he started experimenting with different recipes and ingredients and eventually came up with Peeps.

Peeps, made of marshmallows and coated with colored sugar, were first introduced in 1953 and quickly became a hit among kids and adults alike. Born’s Peeps were known for their soft and fluffy texture, bright colors, and unique shapes. Over the years, Born’s Peeps brand grew and expanded, and today it is a household name that is synonymous with the Easter season.

Bob Born’s contribution to the candy industry cannot be overstated. He changed the way people thought about marshmallows and introduced them in a new and fun way. He was a true innovator and his Peeps have become a staple in the candy aisle during the Easter season. Bob Born was not only a great confectioner but also a visionary who had a clear vision of what he wanted to achieve.

The company now produces 5.5 million Peeps each day using its existing facilities, which is still based on Bob Born’s design. Ross Born, the company’s chief executive officer, recently revealed retirement plans and celebrated the company’s 100th anniversary.

How Did Bob Born Die? What was his Cause of Death

Bob Born, known as the “Father of Peeps,” died at the age of 98 on Sunday, January 29th, 2023. Born was an American confectioner who rose to fame for creating the famous marshmallow treat Peeps, which is widely popular during the Easter season.

The cause of Bob Born’s death has not been revealed by anyone at this time, but it is believed that he died due to a natural cause.

His company, Just Born Quality Confections, a 100-year-old family business announced this sad news through various news networks.
Born Born’s son, Ross Born stated that, “Dad will be remembered most of all as a “genuine mensch”, a person of honor and honesty.”

The city of Bethlehem dedicated February 15 as “Bob Born Day” in 2019 to recognize his enduring contributions to the area.

Bob Born’s death is a great loss to the candy industry and to the world at large. He will always be remembered as the “Father of Peeps” and his contribution to the candy industry will always be appreciated.
