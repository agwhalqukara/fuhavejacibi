Top Facts about Britt Ekland

  • Britt Ekland is a Swedish actress, model and singer.

  • Ekland appeared in films such as The Double Man (1967), The Night They Raided Minsky’s (1968), Machine Gun McCain (1969), Stiletto (1969) and Get Carter (1971).

  • Ekland had a tumultuous marriage with Peter Sellers and was stepmother to his children.
  • She had a son with record producer Lou Adler in 1973.
  • Ekland was romantically linked to rock star Rod Stewart and singer Phil Lewis.
  • Her autobiography True Britt was published in 1980.
